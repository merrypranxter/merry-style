# What This Style Is

## Definition

**New Age Glam Fantasy** is a retro-mystical visual style that merges late-1970s to mid-1980s metaphysical illustration, glam fantasy cinema, soft-focus beauty imagery, prismatic pastel-neon color, and analog media decay into visions of cosmic transcendence.

---

## Why it feels nostalgic but unreal

New Age Glam Fantasy occupies a unique emotional space because it combines:

1. **Actual historical references** (late 70s / early 80s paperback art, VHS era, glam photography)
2. **Dream-logic unreality** (crystals, portals, cosmic temples, impossible lighting)
3. **Memory texture** (analog decay, faded prints, tape softness)

The result is something that feels like a recovered memory of a culture that may never have fully existed—a parallel universe's vision of spiritual glamour.

---

## Where it sits aesthetically

This style lives at the intersection of:

- Metaphysical / spiritual paperback cover art
- 1978–1986 fantasy and sci-fantasy visual language
- Soft diffusion glamour photography
- Cosmic feminine transcendence imagery
- Crystals, mirrors, celestial portals, satin, mist, and rainbow refraction
- Pastel-neon glow systems
- Analog media wear: VHS softness, CRT scanlines, chroma bleed, halation, print fade

---

## The five key words

If you had to describe this style in five words:

**Mystical. Glamorous. Cosmic. Analog. Prismatic.**

---

## What makes it work

The style works when all these elements are present simultaneously:

1. **Symbolic intent** - The image implies revelation, destiny, or transcendence
2. **Glamour language** - Beauty styling, fashion, ceremonial presentation
3. **Soft optics** - Diffusion, bloom, halation, haze
4. **Prismatic color** - Pastel-neon, opalescent, spectral palette
5. **Analog texture** - VHS, CRT, print wear, generational loss
6. **Emotional sincerity** - Earnest wonder rather than ironic detachment

---

## Quick visual checklist

An image is probably New Age Glam Fantasy if it includes:

- [ ] Mystical or cosmic symbolism (crystals, moons, portals, temples)
- [ ] Glamour presentation (styled hair, makeup, jewelry, satin, elegant posing)
- [ ] Soft optical glow (diffusion, bloom, halation, haze)
- [ ] Prismatic pastel-neon color (pink, lavender, cyan, pearl, rainbow refraction)
- [ ] Analog wear texture (VHS softness, CRT scanlines, faded print, chroma bleed)
- [ ] Emotional tone of wonder, initiation, or transcendence

---

## One-sentence summary

**New Age Glam Fantasy is retro mystical glamour with cosmic symbolism, soft-focus bloom, prismatic pastels, and analog decay.**
