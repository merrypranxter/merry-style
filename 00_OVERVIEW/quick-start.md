# Quick Start

## New to New Age Glam Fantasy? Start here.

This guide will get you generating on-model images in minutes.

---

## The 30-second primer

New Age Glam Fantasy = **mystical symbolism + glamour styling + soft optics + prismatic color + analog wear**

Think: lost 1980s metaphysical paperback cover meets VHS fantasy music video meets cosmic priestess glamour shoot.

---

## Your first prompt

Copy and modify this:

```
A radiant oracle with long wild pink curly hair standing in a mirrored crystal temple above reflective black water, surrounded by glowing mushrooms and crystal clusters, wearing an orchid satin gown with silver jewelry, lit by halo backlight through lavender and teal mist, soft diffusion bloom, hot pink violet aqua palette, subtle VHS softness and chroma bleed, composed as a lost metaphysical paperback cover. Style: New Age Glam Fantasy — 1978–1986 metaphysical glam sci-fantasy, diffusion bloom/halation, prismatic pastels, VHS/CRT artifacts, analog film wear.
```

## Prompt building blocks

### Subject options
- radiant oracle / cosmic priestess / moon queen
- mystical heroine with wild pink curls
- crystal temple guardian
- jeweled swan on black water

### Setting options
- mirrored crystal temple
- moonlit garden with glowing moss
- reflective black water landscape
- cosmic desert with aurora sky
- portal staircase in cloud chamber

### Motif options
- crystal clusters
- glowing mushrooms
- moon discs
- rainbow prism refractions
- floating butterflies
- velvet moss

### Styling options
- orchid satin gown
- silver lamé robes
- crystal jewelry
- moon-crown headpiece
- satin gloves
- glossy lips, frosted eyeshadow

### Lighting options
- halo backlight through mist
- moonlit lavender haze
- candy-colored aura spill
- rainbow prism scatter
- lemon-white edge glow

### Palette options
- hot pink, orchid, teal, pearl
- lavender, aqua, lemon, silver
- magenta, turquoise, opal white

### Analog options
- light VHS softness
- subtle chroma bleed
- faint CRT texture
- bloom-heavy highlights
- faded print wear

## Quick style lock line

Always end your prompt with:

```
Style: New Age Glam Fantasy — 1978–1986 metaphysical glam sci-fantasy, diffusion bloom/halation, prismatic pastels, VHS/CRT artifacts, analog film wear.
```

---

## Common mistakes to avoid

| Don't | Do Instead |
|-------|------------|
| "beautiful woman in stars" | "radiant oracle in crystal moon temple" |
| "ethereal glow" | "soft diffusion bloom and halation" |
| "neon lights" | "prismatic pastel-neon aura spill" |
| "sharp 4K render" | "VHS-soft dream-memory texture" |
| "fantasy castle" | "mirrored crystal temple above reflective water" |
| "dark mysterious" | "luminous mystical transcendence" |

---

## 5-minute exercise

1. Pick a subject (oracle, priestess, queen)
2. Pick a setting (crystal temple, moon garden, reflective water)
3. Pick 2-3 motifs (crystals, mushrooms, moon disc)
4. Add glamour styling (satin, jewelry, crown)
5. Add lighting (halo backlight, mist)
6. Add palette (pink, violet, teal)
7. Add analog texture (VHS softness, chroma bleed)
8. End with the style lock line

Generate. Adjust. Repeat.
