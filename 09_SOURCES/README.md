# Sources

This directory will contain the bibliography, reference list, and source citations for New Age Glam Fantasy.

---

## Purpose

New Age Glam Fantasy is a historically grounded aesthetic. The sources section provides:
- Film and television references
- Book and publishing references (metaphysical paperbacks, fantasy covers)
- Photography and visual culture references
- Music and performance references
- Magazine archives
- Academic and critical essays on the aesthetic era

---

## Key source categories

### Film & Television
- Fantasy/sci-fantasy cinema 1978–1986
- Fantasy music videos and specials
- Occult-soft television broadcasts

**Quick reference (from `../01_STYLE_FOUNDATIONS/era-influences.md`):**
- *The NeverEnding Story* (1984)
- *Legend* (1985)
- *Labyrinth* (1986)
- *Flash Gordon* (1980)
- *Xanadu* (1980)
- *The Last Unicorn* (1982)
- Kate Bush music videos
- David Bowie fantasy phases

### Print & Publishing
- Metaphysical and spiritual paperback cover artists
- Fantasy book cover illustrators: Boris Vallejo, Julie Bell, Michael Whelan, Rowena Morrill, Chris Achilleos
- Magazines: Omni, New Age Journal

### Visual Culture
- 70s glam rock photography
- Soft-focus beauty editorial (1978–1986)
- Airbrush art tradition
- Cosmic poster art
