# Lighting

## Purpose of this file

This document defines the lighting logic of **New Age Glam Fantasy**.

Lighting is what turns the palette into atmosphere, the symbolism into revelation, and the glamour into iconography.

---

## Core lighting statement

The lighting of New Age Glam Fantasy is **soft, radiant, ceremonial, atmospheric, prismatic, and emotionally elevated**.

It should feel less like practical illumination and more like:
- aura-light
- moonlight filtered through mist
- halo glow behind a divine figure
- crystal-reflected color
- VHS-bloomed beauty lighting
- cosmic backlight from an unseen portal
- pastel-neon radiance softened by diffusion

---

## The six lighting laws

### 1. Light should feel like a presence
Light often behaves like a symbolic force suggesting awakening, initiation, transformation, divine attention, psychic energy, cosmic alignment, or beauty as revelation.

### 2. Diffusion is essential
Even bright or saturated light should be softened by haze, bloom, mist, halation, lens diffusion, smoke, atmospheric scatter, or VHS softness.

### 3. Backlight is more important than flat front light
This style loves glowing edges, luminous hair halos, moon-disc silhouettes, mist catching light behind the subject, radiant threshold light, and light spilling from portals.

### 4. Colored light should feel cosmetic and celestial
Color in the light should feel like aura spill, prism scatter, glam makeup translated into environment, rainbow refraction, moon-charged pastel neon, satin reflection, or crystal bounce.

### 5. Light should sculpt beauty, not destroy it
The face, hair, skin, hands, jewelry, and fabrics should be treated with glamour logic.

### 6. Brightness should gather around sacred zones
The brightest parts should collect around the face, halo shapes, crystals, portals, moon discs, hands, jewelry, reflective water edges, temple doorways, and aura fields.

---

## Primary lighting modes

### 1. Halo backlight

**Description:** A strong glowing light source sits behind or around the subject, softened by mist, haze, or bloom, creating a divine edge and radiant silhouette.

**Emotional effect:** transcendence, sanctification, icon presence, spiritual arrival, beauty as divinity

**Best uses:** portraits, priestess figures, cover art, moon-disc compositions, portal scenes, ascension imagery

**Prompt language:**
- radiant halo backlight
- moon-disc glow behind the subject
- soft divine backlight through mist
- glowing silhouette edge with heavy bloom
- luminous aura backlight
- celestial rim light and haze

---

### 2. Prism scatter lighting

**Description:** Light is broken through crystal, mirror, glass, or prism surfaces, creating rainbow fragments, pastel flare, and refracted glow.

**Emotional effect:** enchantment, magic, luxury, playfulness, cosmic wonder

**Best uses:** crystal temples, mirrored rooms, beauty close-ups, jewelry shots, maximalist fantasy scenes, music-video imagery

**Prompt language:**
- rainbow prism light scattering across the frame
- refracted crystal highlights
- prismatic flare through mist
- iridescent glass reflections
- spectral light fragments across satin and skin
- crystal-split aura light

---

### 3. Moonlit diffusion lighting

**Description:** Cool, soft, dreamy light with lavender, silver, aqua, or violet bias, filtered through haze or fog and combined with subtle warm beauty highlights.

**Emotional effect:** tenderness, longing, dream-state, psychic stillness, sacred night

**Best uses:** outdoor visionary scenes, reflective water, moon temples, misty portals, moonlit glam portraits

**Prompt language:**
- moonlit lavender haze
- silver-blue diffusion light
- soft violet moon glow through fog
- pearlescent lunar backlight
- aqua-lilac night bloom
- dreamy moonlight with gentle halation

---

### 4. Candy glamour light

**Description:** A brighter, more Lisa Frank–adjacent mode where pink, orchid, teal, aqua, lemon, and pearl highlights interact in a soft-focus glam way.

**Emotional effect:** joy, fantasy excitement, beauty spectacle, pop mysticism, glittery transcendence

**Best uses:** maximalist portraits, VHS music-video imagery, cover art, magical interiors, rainbow temple scenes

**Prompt language:**
- candy-colored aura lighting
- hot pink and teal beauty glow
- orchid and aqua diffusion lighting
- lemon halo edges and magenta bloom
- soft-focus candy neon glamour
- Lisa Frank–adjacent prismatic light spill

---

### 5. Temple threshold lighting

**Description:** A doorway, gate, arch, or portal emits bright symbolic light into a darker or softer surrounding space.

**Emotional effect:** initiation, invitation, destiny, passage, revelation

**Best uses:** temple scenes, portal imagery, staircases, ceremonial arrivals, iconic wide compositions

**Prompt language:**
- blinding temple doorway glow through mist
- radiant threshold light
- portal light spilling across reflective floors
- sacred entrance bloom
- luminous gate with soft fog diffusion
- horizon portal shining into haze

---

### 6. Reflective water / mirror spill lighting

**Description:** Light bounces upward or outward from glossy black water, mirror floors, mirrored walls, or reflective surfaces, adding surreal softness and doubling the glow.

**Emotional effect:** infinity, dream logic, metaphysical depth, elegance, emotional richness

**Best uses:** temple interiors, reflective black water, beauty icon scenes, mirrored corridors, cosmic landscape compositions

**Prompt language:**
- reflective black water catching pink and violet glow
- mirror-floor light spill
- doubled aura reflections
- silvered reflections in mist
- teal and magenta light rippling across dark water
- mirrored light bloom beneath the figure

---

## Lighting color bias

This style usually prefers lighting built from:
- hot pink, bubblegum pink, magenta, coral-pink
- lavender, lilac, orchid, violet
- teal, aqua, turquoise, mint
- lemon glow, pearl white, silver, champagne light

**Usually deprioritizes:**
- red-heavy lighting
- royal blue dominance
- green-heavy industrial light
- orange sodium-vapor realism
- stark white clinical light

## Default lighting recommendation

If no specific lighting setup is requested, a strong default is:
- soft halo backlight
- gentle frontal beauty fill
- pink / orchid / teal aura spill
- mist in the air
- bloom around highlights
- reflective silver or water bounce
- slight prism flare
- VHS-soft clipping on the brightest edges
