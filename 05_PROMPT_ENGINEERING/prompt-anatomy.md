# Prompt Anatomy

## Purpose of this file

This document breaks down how New Age Glam Fantasy prompts are built — layer by layer — so you can construct prompts that reliably hit the style, regardless of which AI platform you use.

---

## The ten prompt layers

A fully specified New Age Glam Fantasy prompt is built from ten sequential layers.

You do not always need all ten. Short prompts can work with four or five. But understanding each layer helps you diagnose what is missing when results drift off-model.

```
1. SUBJECT
2. SETTING
3. MOTIFS
4. STYLING
5. LIGHTING
6. OPTICS
7. PALETTE
8. ANALOG ARTIFACTS
9. EMOTIONAL TONE
10. FORMAT + STYLE LOCK
```

---

## Layer 1: Subject

The central figure or object the image is built around.

### What to include
- identity type (oracle, priestess, queen, guardian, cosmic heroine)
- key visual trait (wild pink curls, luminous skin, jeweled crown)
- action or posture (standing, gazing, ascending, performing ritual)

### Strong vs weak subject language

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| "a woman" | "a radiant oracle with long wild pink curly hair" |
| "a figure" | "a cosmic priestess with silver jewelry and frosted eyeshadow" |
| "a goddess" | "a moon queen with blue eyes and gloss lips, draped in orchid satin" |

### Prompt examples
- A luminous oracle with long wild pink curls standing barefoot
- A cosmic priestess with silver moon-crown and satin gloves
- A jeweled guardian figure with aura light radiating behind her

---

## Layer 2: Setting

The environment the subject inhabits.

### What to include
- environment type (temple, garden, landscape, chamber, water)
- material/atmosphere (crystal, mirror, reflective water, mist, fog)
- scale cue (vast, intimate, architectural, cosmic)

### Strong setting language
- a mirrored crystal temple above reflective black water
- a moonlit garden filled with glowing moss and pastel mushrooms
- a cloud chamber with floating crystal staircases
- a vast celestial desert under aurora sky
- a prism-lit ceremonial hall with silver drapery

---

## Layer 3: Motifs

Symbolic objects and recurring visual elements that give the image metaphysical charge.

### What to include
- 2–4 motifs drawn from the style's symbolic vocabulary
- crystal forms, celestial bodies, initiatory objects, devotional symbols, organic sacred elements

### Motif clusters that work well together

**Crystal temple cluster:**
crystal clusters + mirrored pillars + reflective floor + prism light

**Moon garden cluster:**
glowing moss + pastel mushrooms + moon disc + reflective pool

**Cosmic initiation cluster:**
portal + glowing staircase + aura rings + silver robes

**Glam ecology cluster:**
jewel-toned butterflies + velvet moss + crystal dew + rainbow refraction

### Prompt language
- surrounded by crystal clusters and floating prism shards
- glowing mushrooms rising from velvet moss
- moon disc rising behind the figure with halo diffusion
- rainbow prism refractions crossing the frame

---

## Layer 4: Styling

The glamour and fashion language of the subject.

### What to include
- textile and material (satin, lamé, chiffon, silk, sequins)
- garment type (gown, robes, cape, veils)
- accessory detail (jewelry, crown, satin gloves, crystal ring)
- makeup and hair (glossy lips, frosted eyeshadow, voluminous pink curls)

### Styling rules
- All styling should feel **ceremonial**, not casual or contemporary
- Fabric choices should support light behavior (satin reflects, chiffon diffuses, lamé sparkles)
- Jewelry and hair are as important as clothing

### Prompt language
- wearing an orchid satin gown with silver lamé trim and chiffon veils
- jeweled moon-crown headpiece, satin gloves, crystal pendant
- glossy lips, frosted lavender eyeshadow, voluminous hair with shimmer

---

## Layer 5: Lighting

The light source behavior and color.

### Primary lighting modes
- halo backlight through mist
- moonlit lavender haze
- candy-colored aura spill
- rainbow prism scatter
- lemon-white edge glow
- portal light spilling into fog

### Lighting rules
- Backlight is almost always present
- Light should have color bias from the style palette (pink, lavender, teal, aqua, lemon)
- Diffusion and bloom should be implied in the lighting description

### Prompt language
- lit by radiant halo backlight through pink and teal mist
- moonlit lavender haze filling the chamber
- candy-colored aura lighting in orchid, aqua, and lemon

---

## Layer 6: Optics

The optical characteristics that create the dream-state feel.

### What to include
- diffusion type (soft focus, halation, bloom)
- lens quality (haze, dreamy, romantic, prismatic)
- focus handling (softened detail, atmospheric blur)

### Optics rules
- Always include at least one optics modifier
- "Soft" and "dreamy" alone are too vague — add specificity
- Prism flare can be added as an optics element

### Prompt language
- heavy diffusion bloom and dreamy halation
- mist-filtered highlights with pearlescent overexposure
- soft optical haze with prismatic lens flare
- glowing lens softness and romantic analog diffusion
- soft-focus glam optics with VHS-warm blur

---

## Layer 7: Palette

The explicit color specification.

### What to include
- 3–5 dominant hues from the style palette
- a neutral anchor (pearl white, moon silver, opal)
- a shorthand name if helpful (Lisa Frank–adjacent, prismatic pastels)

### Palette rules
- Name specific hues — "pink" alone is too vague
- Always include at least one neutral alongside the saturated tones
- Rainbow/prismatic language is valid but should feel refracted, not flat

### Prompt language
- hot pink, violet, teal, aqua, and pearl white palette
- Lisa Frank–adjacent cosmic palette of bubblegum pink, orchid, turquoise, lemon, and silver
- opalescent pastel-neon atmosphere in lavender, aqua, and champagne gold
- prismatic pastels softened by VHS bloom

---

## Layer 8: Analog Artifacts

The media wear and reproduction texture.

### What to include
- VHS / tape characteristics
- CRT / scanline texture
- print-based characteristics
- chroma behavior

### Analog levels

| Level | Best for | Phrase |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Whispered (1) | Elegant portraits, subtle variants | "slight bloom, faint CRT texture" |
| Clear (2) | Most default work, covers, temple scenes | "VHS softness, chroma bleed, light scanlines" |
| Heavy (3) | Explicit VHS / lost media vibes | "obvious tape blur, strong bloom, visible scan texture" |

### Prompt language
- subtle VHS softness and gentle chroma bleed
- faint CRT scanline texture and bloom-heavy highlights
- light analog print fade and tape memory softness
- dreamy generational copy loss with magenta-cyan drift

---

## Layer 9: Emotional Tone

The feeling the image is meant to evoke.

### Core emotional states
- transcendence / sacred glamour
- cosmic initiation / visionary romance
- enchantment / awakening
- luxurious spiritual revelation
- dream-memory nostalgia

### Tone rules
- Sincerity is essential — avoid ironic distance
- Even maximalist images should feel emotionally committed
- One or two specific emotional words beat a long list of adjectives

### Prompt language
- evoking sacred beauty and cosmic initiation
- dreamy sacred longing
- luxurious spiritual revelation
- mystic glamour and visionary serenity

---

## Layer 10: Format and Style Lock

The compositional framing and the canonical style tag.

### Format cues
- composed like a lost metaphysical paperback cover
- framed as an 80s fantasy film still
- symmetrical devotional icon composition
- center-framed glam oracle portrait
- wide cinematic moon temple scene
- VHS-soft music-video frame

### Style lock line
Always end your prompt with the style lock line:

```
Style: New Age Glam Fantasy — 1978–1986 metaphysical glam sci-fantasy, diffusion bloom/halation, prismatic pastels, VHS/CRT artifacts, analog film wear.
```

This is the single most important phrase for keeping model outputs on-style.

```
A cosmic priestess in a mirrored crystal temple above reflective black water, glowing mushrooms and crystal clusters, halo backlight through mist, soft diffusion bloom, hot pink violet aqua palette, subtle VHS softness, composed as a lost metaphysical paperback cover. Style: New Age Glam Fantasy — 1978–1986 metaphysical glam sci-fantasy, diffusion bloom/halation, prismatic pastels, VHS/CRT artifacts, analog film wear.
```

A radiant oracle with long wild pink curly hair standing in a mirrored crystal temple above reflective black water, surrounded by glowing mushrooms and crystal clusters and drifting prism flares, wearing an orchid satin gown with silver jewelry and chiffon veils, lit by halo backlight through lavender and teal haze, soft-focus glam optics with prismatic lens flare, Lisa Frank–adjacent hot pink violet aqua lemon and pearl palette, faint CRT texture and chroma bleed, evoking sacred glamour and cosmic initiation, composed as a lost metaphysical paperback cover. ## Prompt troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Too modern / clean | Add: era cues, VHS softness, "1978–1986", paperback framing |
| Too cyberpunk | Remove: urban/tech language. Add: mist, moonlight, crystals, pastel tones |
| Too dark / horror | Add: pearl glow, pink/lavender, devotional tone. Remove: dread, shadow-heavy wording |
| Too generic ethereal | Add: specific motifs, tactile materials, era anchors, symbolic structure |
| Too synthwave | Soften palette, add mist and bloom, replace grid/car language with temple/crystal |
| Lost glamour | Add: satin, jewelry, styled hair, makeup, iconic pose language |
| Lost symbolism | Add: specific motifs (crystals, moon disc, portal), metaphysical framing |
| Palette wrong | Name specific hues from the style palette, add analog softness to color description |
