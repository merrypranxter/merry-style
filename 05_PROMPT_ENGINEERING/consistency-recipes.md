# Consistency Recipes

## Purpose of this file

Consistency recipes are reusable prompt formulas that reliably produce on-model New Age Glam Fantasy images. Each recipe is a tested, complete structure you can copy, modify the variables, and use immediately.

Think of them as starting templates — they establish the style, then you customize the subject and setting details.

---

## How to use recipes

1. Copy the recipe
2. Replace the bracketed `[variables]` with your chosen subject, setting, and motifs
3. Adjust any details you want to change
4. Do not remove the style lock line at the end
5. Generate, evaluate, iterate

---
