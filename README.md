# New Age Glam Fantasy Style Bible

A research, reference, and prompt-engineering repository dedicated to the visual language of **New Age Glam Fantasy**: a hybrid aesthetic combining **late-1970s to mid-1980s metaphysical paperback art**, **glam sci-fantasy cinema optics**, **soft-focus fashion surrealism**, **prismatic pastel-neon maximalism**, and **VHS/CRT analog decay**.

This repository exists to document, define, compare, and operationalize the style so it can be **studied, recreated, prompted, art-directed, and expanded consistently**.

---

## What is New Age Glam Fantasy?

New Age Glam Fantasy is a luminous retro-futurist mystical aesthetic built from the overlap of:
- metaphysical / spiritual paperback cover art
- 1978–1986 fantasy and sci-fantasy visual language
- soft diffusion glamour photography
- cosmic feminine transcendence imagery
- crystals, mirrors, celestial portals, satin, mist, and rainbow refraction
- pastel-neon glow systems
- analog media wear: VHS softness, CRT scanlines, chroma bleed, halation, print fade

It should feel like a **lost metaphysical bestseller cover**, an **80s fantasy film still**, a **luxury occult music video frame**, or a **visionary paperback illustration remembered through tape degradation**.

## Style pillars

The style is built from seven recurring pillars:

### 1. Mystical metaphysical imagery
Crystals, portals, moons, pyramids, temples, auras, veils, divine light, psychic symbolism.

### 2. Glamour language
Gloss, satin, metallics, styled hair, dramatic makeup, jewelry, elegant poses, cinematic beauty lighting.

### 3. Soft optical diffusion
Halation, bloom, lens haze, pearlescent highlights, glowing edges, dreamlike softness.

### 4. Prismatic color
Pink, lavender, cyan, opal, ice blue, peach, pearl white, gold, spectral rainbow flare.

### 5. Analog wear
Tape softness, color drift, scanlines, chroma separation, print fade, mild distortion, generational loss.

### 6. Cosmic environments
Star fields, cloud chambers, celestial desert horizons, reflective black water, glowing interiors, mirrored voids.

### 7. Emotional transcendence
Initiation, enchantment, spiritual glamour, destiny, romance, visionary revelation, sacred drama.

---

## What this style is not

This is one of the most important parts of the repo.

New Age Glam Fantasy is **not**:
- generic fantasy art
- dark occult horror
- cyberpunk neon
- synthwave outrun by default
- vaporwave collage nostalgia
- clean contemporary luxury surrealism
- Y2K chrome futurism
- sterile AI "ethereal woman in space" imagery
- maximalism without symbolism
- spirituality without glamour
- glamour without mysticism
- retro without diffusion and analog softness

The style requires the **coexistence** of mysticism, glamour, cosmic symbolism, and analog imperfection.

## Prompting philosophy

This style works best when prompted as a layered visual system, not a pile of random adjectives.

A strong prompt usually includes:
- subject
- environment
- symbolic motifs
- era cues
- lighting cues
- optical softness
- palette cues
- analog artifact layer
- emotional tone
- composition or framing cue
- finish / medium cue

### Basic skeleton

```
[subject] in [environment], surrounded by [motifs], styled with [fashion/material/era cues], lit by [lighting], shot with [optical cues], in [palette], with [artifact layer], evoking [emotion/theme], composed as [format/composition cue].
```

### Style line

```
Style: New Age Glam Fantasy — 1978–1986 metaphysical glam sci-fantasy, diffusion bloom/halation, prismatic pastels, VHS/CRT artifacts, analog film wear.
```

### Example prompt

```
A radiant woman with long wild pink curls standing in a mirrored crystal temple above reflective black water, moon halo behind her, satin gloves, silver jewelry, pastel lavender and opal cyan atmosphere, soft diffusion bloom, pearlescent skin highlights, rainbow prism refractions crossing the frame, light VHS chroma bleed, faint CRT scanline texture, dreamy metaphysical paperback cover composition, spiritual glamour, cosmic initiation.

Style: New Age Glam Fantasy — 1978–1986 metaphysical glam sci-fantasy, diffusion bloom/halation, prismatic pastels, VHS/CRT artifacts, analog film wear.
```

## Design principles

To keep the style coherent, prioritize:
- softness over sharpness
- glow over contrast harshness
- symbolism over randomness
- atmosphere over plain depiction
- glamour over plain costume
- prismatic color over flat color
- analog imperfection over sterile polish
- emotional transcendence over empty prettiness

---

## Common failure modes

The style usually breaks when it becomes:
- too digital-clean
- too cyberpunk
- too horror-occult
- too generic "celestial"
- too modern-fashion editorial
- too monochrome
- too hard-lit
- too literal sci-fi
- too cluttered without hierarchy
- too AI-glossy without analog damage
